Air Freight

Air freight services that are ALL IN for you.

Looking for air freight services that deliver excellence and value? Express Courier Limited is the answer. Express Courier Limited provides a variety of solutions to meet your unique air freight logistics needs. Our team of air freight experts provide timely air freight services that range from time critical next flight out to affordable value air for less critical shipments. Your dedicated account manager is ready to hear from you any time of the day 24/7/365.

Leaders in time critical air freight services.

When time is of the essence, we offer our clients a variety of time critical air freight services.
Let our team recommend the one that best fits your schedule. Our mission is to deliver your time sensitive shipments to the other side of the country or the other side of the globe. All in a timely manner.

Next Flight Out services in the nick of time.

One of the many time critical air freight services we provide is Next Flight Out (NFO) logistics. This is the perfect solution when a customer is looking for the quickest possible transit of their urgent shipments. Our NFO service cuts to the chase. With an NFO shipment, we have one of our couriers pick up your package from your specified location and deliver it to the next available airline with the fastest, most efficient route to your destination. Once the package arrives at its destination, another one of our couriers will pick it up from the airport and deliver it to its final location. Or, if you prefer, you can have it held at the airport for pickup. Our NFO air freight service is perfect for the company desiring to avoid downtime and delays in their supply chain.

Hand Carry / On Board Courier for hands-on reliability.

Oftentimes, your most precious cargo requires a special level of care. Our hand carry / on board courier service provides your parcels with an extra degree of door-to-door or airport-to-airport certainty and security. We put your shipment in the hands of our thoroughly vetted professional couriers. A trained employee will pick up your package, board a passenger or chartered jet, and hand carry your delivery to its destination. This ensures unmatched chain of custody because the shipment never leaves the hands of our courier until its delivered.

Charter aircraft when regular airline schedules arent the right fit.

Your shipment doesnt have to be at the mercy of airline schedules. Time-critical parcels deserve special measures. If the commercial airlines dont have service to your packages destination or their timetables are not timely, we will charter an aircraft to get the job done. After all, some shipments are just that important.

Time critical solutions both domestically and internationally.

Express Courier Limited is proud to be an International Air Transport Association (IATA) agent for all major airlines. We offer a complete package of air freight services through our global network of logistics experts and innovative technology. Our suite of air freight services saves customers from the challenge of facilitating multiple carriers and sifting through laborious and often confusing customs documents.
