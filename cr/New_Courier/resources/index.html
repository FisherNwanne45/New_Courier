CASE STUDIES

A unique logistics solution for a unique electronics challenge.
Our client was a global electronics manufacturer in need of a very unique logistics solution.

The Challenge

Our clients challenge was clear. They needed to deliver new digital projection systems to thousands of theaters across the United States. The theaters would need to be thoroughly and painstakingly retrofitted to adapt to the new technology. All in a small window of time.

The Solution

Express Courier Limited managed delivery capability coordinated the 4-person install teams to collaborate with the electronics companys field engineers and theatre personnel at each uniquely configured location. A path in and path out logistical solution was developed with precise asset serialization of the new equipment. This thorough preplanning resulted in a smooth and timely install with no loss of revenue for the theatres. Thorough reverse logistics delivered the legacy projectors for responsible recycling. The successful outcome was the result of Express Courier Limited's stringent pre-calls, robust site surveys, and detailed planning.

The Client Speaks

The logistical requirements for these digital projector conversions are very complex and require a lot of this equipment. But through collaboration and experts working with experts we got the job done. Every single theatre.
Supply chain problem solving with life-giving results.
When a mammoth logistics problem requires precise supply chain problem solving, medical manufacturers turn to Express Courier Limited.

The Challenge

Our client needed a centrally located distribution center close to a major airport for 5,000 skus. All with a cold chain environment and flexible pricing. Forward and reverse logistics would be essential.

The Solution

Express Courier Limited collaborated with our medical client to achieve ERP conversions and super frozen storage for these medical products. Our thorough warehouse management system continues to deliver total flexibility and unparalleled accuracy. Our collective participation in Kaizen events along with the implementation of Kanban inventory management principles ensures a continuous improvement cycle.

The Client Speaks

Express Courier Limited has shown year after year they are an asset to our operation by providing a lean and cost-conscious service, able to deliver consistent budget control.
Meeting the unique needs of a growing security technology company.
Express Courier Limited took the time to listen and understand. Then we delivered an end-to-end solution unique to this security technology clients needs.

The Challenge

This client required a total solution that could encompass supply chain and return logistics. 3-8 skids of home alarm inventory needed to get to 120 field offices throughout the U.S. Deliveries would need to be made to residential, hotels, apartments, as well as storage facilities. Deliveries needed to provide for lift gate, pallet jack and inside logistics. Each appointment would require a call 2 hours prior to delivery. The return logistics would require pick up within a 2-hour window, involving pallets, shrink-wrap and tape. Returned goods would encompass palletizing on site, truck loading and an accurate count. Return goods must arrive back at warehouse within 3-4 days.

The Solution

Express Courier Limited organized the outbound order electronically. Our logistics professionals assembled a dedicated team to coordinate outbound shipments and a separate team for inbound merchandise. We established a discreet email address to communicate with field offices and sent reports morning and afternoon declaring a shipments status. Our comprehensive solution meant that field reps were no longer required to wait for goods to appear and that the corporate status. This also meant that the corporate office would no longer need to receive unnecessary calls inquiring about a shipments status.

The Client Speaks

Express Courier Limited took the time to understand our needs. Their innovative team of logistical experts worked around the clock to deliver an outside-the-box custom solution. They reduced our product to field transit time from a 7-day average to 3.4 days! Express Courier Limited gets it and gets it done!
